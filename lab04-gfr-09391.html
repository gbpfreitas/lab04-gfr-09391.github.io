360px - Mobile

For mobile devices 360px was choosen because the majority of the smartphones has that resolution as a pattern, such as iPhone 6/7/8 and Galaxy Note 3.

768px - Tablets

For Tablets devices 768px was choosen because that is usually the tablet resolution pattern, according to screensiz.es.

1200px - Desktop and Laptop

According to the Designer Insights website, a desktop/laptop browser usually demands, on average, a minimum of 1200px for its content.
